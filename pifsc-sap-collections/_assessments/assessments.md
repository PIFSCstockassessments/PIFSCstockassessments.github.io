Fish [stock assessments](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/topic/population-assessments#fish-stocks) are scientific analyses that provide fisheries managers with information on a fish and fishery, including how many fish there are and how many can be harvested sustainably.

We use [statistical models](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/feature-story/fish-stock-assessment-101-part-2-closer-look-stock-assessment-models) to integrate fishery, biology, and oceanography in order to describe how fish populations are responding to fishery harvest. Our models produce estimates that are the basis for domestic and international fishery regulations in the Pacific.
